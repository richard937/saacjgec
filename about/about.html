About Us

Space and Aeronautics Activity Centre is a platform Where every interested students of our college can explore their talent, also can collect knowledge of Space science and Technology and get practical experiences about Aerospace and Aeronautics technology. Aerospace is a developing topic and in India it need a huge development. A lot of opportunities and research topics are here. So we initiate to make a active club or society of Aerospace where can access more data about space and surrounding technology to spread the mind set to future JOLITES of enhancing personal and SAAC’s advancement. Our Respected Alumni and pass out seniors (related and well designed with this brunch) are collaborating us in this motive and also many of the associated Professors from every dept. are hopefully and interest fully agree to collaborate our efforts in this field. Finally we want to generate Aerospace culture and Aerospace Research and Development in our college. This society also provides practical experiences and Project engagement on this field. Seminars, Workshops, GD, topic wise classes will be held side by side. It is the subject where Every departments can join (ME, CE, EE, ECE, CSE, IT, math, physics, chemistry, economy, statistics, management etc.). Hope all the collaboration and student efforts will lead this Team to the grand success.
