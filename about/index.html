About Us

Space and Aeronautics Activity Center(SAAC) is an initiative of a few energetic students from Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College who dream of reaching space someday!

We're an active community of students showcasing and testing our skills in projects ranging from Model Rocket Propulsion to developing Flight Guidance System. We idealize great personalities like APJ Abdul Kalam, Nambi Narayanan, Vikram Sarabhai, Elon Musk, Chris Hadfield and others.

Our Vision

We dream about growing to a team as strong as NASA, ISRO or Spacex in future and bring significant help to the challenges of space. Also, we want to make it as the first stop for beginners interested in Space Science.

Our goal is to provide the community members with not only theoretical knowledge but also practical experiences about Aerospace and Aeronautics Technology. Bear with us and we'll get there one day!



Collaboration

As there is a saying
"If you wanna go fast, go alone. If you wanna go far, go together."

So we welcome collaborators, mentors and students across the nation to bring in new ideas for the greater good.

Project engagements, seminars, workshops, online and offline classes strengthens our community bond and helping us to grow further and beyond.




Our Divisions

Management Team

The Management Team of SAAC is active in taking all the important decisions for the welfare of SAAC. The management team comprises of very sharp and creative minds who are ready to take SAAC to any level possible.

Software Team

Software Team is at the core of the SAAC, bringing the face of SAAC to the world. The team is also active in solving problems from Automated Flight System to Remote Guidance and Flight Monitoring.

Technical Team

The Technical Team comprises of both the hardware and software teams. The team is responsible for bringing all the ideas to reality! The Team has some really young and curious minds, ready to take up any challenge along the way.

Marketing Team

The Marketing Team is responsible for the far reach of SAAC. The team creates some of the awesome content which our audience cannot resist watching. This team makes the SAAC feel alive and forever fresh.
